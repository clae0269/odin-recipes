Gordon Ramsay's Idiot Sandwich

Gordon Ramsays iconic idiot sandwich, recipe was originally crafted in an episode of Hell's Cafeteria and has since been used in countless of meme references.

Ingredients

Serves one large portion

  • 1 idiot
  • 2 pieces of bread

Steps

  1. Find yourself an idiot
  2. Take your 2 pieces of bread and mash in the idiot between the 2 pieces of bread
  3. Ask the person what they are, where they'll reply: ''an idiot sandwich''
  4. And there u go! You've now made yourself, an idiot sandwich
